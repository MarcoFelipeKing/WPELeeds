About the Competition

The WPE Photo Competition was established in 2021 to celebrate the visual dimensions of our research and to foster creative engagement with environmental themes among our community of academics, researchers, and students.

Each year, participants are invited to submit photographs that connect to a selected theme relevant to water, public health, or environmental research. The competition highlights both the scientific and artistic perspectives on our work, creating a unique platform where technical expertise meets creative expression.

Winning entries are showcased on our website, featured in the department newsletter, shared on our social media channels, and displayed in the WPE building.

Submission Guidelines

Eligibility

Open to all WPE members - academics, PhD students, postdocs, staff, and research affiliates.

Photo Requirements

Minimum resolution of 3000 x 2000 pixels (6 megapixels). Accepted formats: JPEG or PNG. Basic editing allowed, but no heavy manipulation or AI-generated images.

Documentation

Each submission must include a title, location where the photo was taken, brief description (max 100 words), and connection to the competition theme.

Ethics

Photos featuring identifiable people must have appropriate consent. Wildlife and environmental photography must adhere to ethical standards (no disturbance of wildlife or protected areas).

Rights

Photographers retain copyright of their images. By submitting, you grant WPE permission to use your photo in department publications, website, and social media with appropriate credit.

Submit Your Photo

How to Submit

Submissions for the 2025 WPE Photo Competition will open on March 1, 2025. Each participant may submit up to three photographs (maximum one per category).

To submit your entry, you will need:

  • Your high-resolution photograph(s)
  • Title and description for each photo
  • Information about where and when the photo was taken
  • Category selection
  • Brief statement on how your photo connects to the theme

If your photograph features identifiable people, you'll need to provide confirmation that you have obtained appropriate consent.
